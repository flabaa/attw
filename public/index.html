About Us

Welcome to

"Awaken to the World"

The purpose of “Awaken to the World” is to mobilize the Church in Ghana to reach the world. More than 3500 pastors and church leaders have attended the programme in Accra, Kumasi, and Tema since it began since 2005, and they are working with their churches across the country.

"Awaken to the World" (ATTW) is affiliated to Pentecost University. Participants who attend two sessions of ATTW are eligible for one year of credit at that university, which is fully accredited, has its own charter, and grants its own degrees.
